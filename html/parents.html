Parents at ALU

Choosing a university is perhaps the biggest decision you’ve had to work through with your child. And though he/she will be the one doing the studying, you too have a major stake in
his/her choice of a university.

Parents Weekend→

African Leadership University (ALU) is committed to creating ethical and entrepreneurial leaders who learn through experience. That’s why we decided to create Parents Weekend, so the families of our students can experience for themselves what it’s like to be a student here.

 

Support at ALU→

Thanks to the generous support of parents like you, ALU is able to provide strong financial aid packages, world-class teaching and innovative programming that helps purposeful students develop the skills and the confidence to transform Africa.

Contact Us→

If you would like to attend one of our future events or are interested in becoming a Parent Ambassador, feel free to reach out Bijoux Essuman at nessuman@alueducation.com. We would love to have you become part of the change happening at ALU!

ABOUT ALU

ALU aims to develop 3 million ethical and entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and the world by 2035. It uses a personalized, student-driven, project-based, and mission-oriented approach to create agile, lifelong learners who can adapt to a changing world. ALU's first site was inaugurated in September 2015 in Mauritius. Its second campus was inaugurated in September 2017 in Kigali Heights, Rwanda.
