Academic Overview

The ALU Undergraduate Programme is designed with your future in mind.
We believe in preparing our students for the world of tomorrow filled with new ideas, big challenges and jobs that haven’t even been created yet.
With that in mind, we have crafted a learning experience that allows you to continually apply the skills you learn, both in and out of the classroom and in everything you do.

ALU Difference→

Everyone’s different—including you.
That’s why at ALU, we don’t do “one size fits all” education.

We build the skills that you, as a young leader and entrepreneur will need for the future.

BSc(Hons) Computing Degree→

Our computing programme provides students with the computational reasoning needed to excel in programming, web and mobile development, and software engineering. In addition to technical skills, students study the ethical, social, economical and environmental impact of computing to develop robust solutions to local and global challenges.The degree is 3 years with each year bringing different courses for students to explore.

ABOUT ALU

ALU aims to develop 3 million ethical and entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and the world by 2035. It uses a personalized, student-driven, project-based, and mission-oriented approach to create agile, lifelong learners who can adapt to a changing world. ALU's first site was inaugurated in September 2015 in Mauritius. Its second campus was inaugurated in September 2017 in Kigali Heights, Rwanda.
